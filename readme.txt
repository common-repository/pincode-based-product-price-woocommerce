== Description ==

**Pincode Based Product Price Woocommerce** allows you to sell the same product in multiple price based on the pincode/zipcode of the customer.

WooCommerce 2.6.0 or later.

- **Database Management:** Management of a comprehensive database containing various PIN codes, associated regions, and corresponding pricing structures for efficient calculations.
- **Compatibility and Integration:** Integration of this feature with WooCommerce seamlessly, ensuring compatibility with the existing e-commerce platform and its functionalities.
- **Shipping and Tax Calculations:** Adjustments in shipping rates and tax calculations based on the entered PIN code, providing accurate cost estimations to customers.
- **Conditional Logic:** Applying conditional rules or logic to offer discounts, promotions, or special pricing based on specific PIN codes or regions.

== Installation ==

1. Download, install and activate the plugin.
2. Go to Products -> Add/Edit Product
3. Inside Product Pincode price metabox , you assign the price.
